Day 2  (28/01/2023)

CODE-TECH

Rules

  1. Programming languages will be used as 'C', Python, JavaScript(JS).
  2. Participants whose code gets executed will be given higher Preference.
  3. The second preference will be given to code with least number of errors.
  4. In case of tie in the number of errors then timing will be considered.

TREASURE HUNT

Rules

  1. It will be a solo event.
  2. The winner is the first person to discover the treasure, followed by the second
  3. Rules may be changed to meet specific needs (you will be informed after successful registration).
  4. In order to complete the rounds and reach the treasure,you must understand the hints that are given on each round.

BLINDTYPING

Rules

  1. Participants have to type a program with monitor off.
  2. Eachround will have an increasing difficulty.
  3. Participants must complete a round to move on to the next.
  4. The typing will be judge dont he basis of accuracy and time taken
  5. In the case of a tie, the winner will be decided based on the least number of errors and submission time.
